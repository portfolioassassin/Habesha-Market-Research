Methodology

How a Thesis
Gets Written.

Each Substack report is the visible tip of a deeper research process — three stages, weeks of compounding before publication.

◈

Frame the Sector

Start at the sector level — secular drivers, capital cycle, where supply and demand are about to dislocate. The frame decides which names are worth a deep dive at all.

⟁

Build the Model

From SEC filings, transcripts, channel checks, and unit-economics math — what does the next three to five years actually look like, and where is the consensus wrong? Numbers come before narrative.

◎

Open the Position

The thesis only ships after a sized position is live in the paper book — entries, stops, catalyst calendar, and exit triggers all written down. No hidden conviction; the trade IS the receipt.
