Methodology

How a thesis
gets written.

Each Substack report is the visible tip of a deeper research process — three stages, weeks of compounding before publication. Numbers come before narrative.

01 / FRAME
◈

Frame the sector

Start at the sector level — secular drivers, capital cycle, where supply and demand are about to dislocate. The frame decides which names are worth a deep dive at all.

02 / BUILD
⟁

Build the model

From SEC filings, transcripts, channel checks, and unit-economics math — what does the next three to five years actually look like, and where is consensus wrong?

03 / SHIP
◎

Open the position

The thesis only ships after a sized position is live in the paper book — entries, stops, catalyst calendar, exit triggers all written down. The trade IS the receipt.
